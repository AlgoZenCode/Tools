How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compression tool makes it easy to reduce file sizes while maintaining visual quality. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher values = better quality)
  3. Optionally resize the image or change the output format
  4. Click "Compress Image" to process your file
  5. Download the optimized image when processing is complete

Why Compress Your Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading: Smaller images load quicker, improving user experience and SEO rankings
  • Reduced bandwidth usage: Save on hosting costs and data transfers
  • Better email delivery: Compressed images are easier to send as attachments
  • Optimized social media: Many platforms compress images anyway - do it better yourself
